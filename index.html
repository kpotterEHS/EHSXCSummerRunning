Target Training Zones ⏱️

Select your current 5K time trial or race fitness level to see your precise training paces.

Zone 2: Aerobic Base Speed 🌱

Builds Aerobic Capacity

This is the engine builder. Your breathing must be comfortable and completely conversational throughout the entire run. Keep your heart rate relaxed to stimulate capillary density, mitochondria growth, and cardiac stroke volume adaptations.

Your Target Zone 2 Pace
8:05 - 8:55 / mile
(5:01 - 5:32 / km)
Zone 3: Lactate Threshold Speed ⏱️

Improves Fatigue Resistance

This is your "comfortably hard" tempo effort. It teaches your muscles to clear lactic acid quickly during sustained efforts. Avoid turning this into an all-out race; you should feel steady, controlled, and able to only speak in brief, clipped 3-4 word sentences.

Your Target Threshold Pace
6:50 - 7:00 / mile
(4:15 - 4:21 / km)
